In the name of the Pharaoh Mernefer, son of the great Re, shining like the sun
in the sky, protector of the upper and lower land, who rules with unshakable
power and wisdom, feared by enemies and friends alike, first among kings,
deserving eternal praise, in the thirtieth year of his reign.

When the glorious Mernefer, under the protection of the mighty Osiris, defeated
in that fateful moment the treacherous Hyksos, who invaded the upper land in
the name of Seth and sought to divide it.  Step by step, Mernefer freed Egypt
and restored its integrity. The scattered Hyksos lost many chariots, the bodies
of enemies covered the entire desert. After the victory, Mernefer turned to
Isis and performed a powerful ritual under the myrtle trees using magic.

The first part of Mernefer's life is over, he is no longer among the living.
His body is buried deep in a sarcophagus in Saqqara, at the burial place of all
kings, returned back to the heart of the upper land, to embark on a long
journey. There his wife, Shepseskare, awaits her beloved husband, before they
leave to the realm of the dead together.

Glory to the Mernefer, king of the kings, may he find rest and peace.
